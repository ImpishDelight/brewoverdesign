A semi-annual meet-up for designers of all shapes and sizes.

No workshops, no presentations, no set agenda. Just you, a brew and like-minded pixel-tweakers.

Brew Over Design is a semi-annual event to strengthen the Kitchener-Waterloo design community. It's the chance to meet with your peers, share your work, get feedback, and share expertise in a casual atmosphere (plus, booze).

If your throat needs a break from all the shop-talk, there will also be design-spirited contests, portfolio review sessions with local designers, art by local artists for sale, and who knows what else afoot.

All design-minded folk are welcome. Come all ye graphic designers, ye user experience architects, ye designer/analysts and everyone in-between. You're among friends.

Four local designers will be joining us to share their expertise and review portfolios. Spots are limited and sign-up begins at 7pm at the door.

Philip Mondor Partner and Creative Director Studio Locale

Phil has worked across a number of different sectors, with start-ups to well established organizations delivering award winning brands, websites, packaging and all the ever-evolving pieces that you might need.

Kyra Kendall Illustrator/Designer Freelance

Kyra Kendall is a freelance Illustrator and Designer who has had the honour of developing some amazing mobile gaming, package design, and illustration work for a vast array of clients. Prior to being a freelancer, Kyra worked for MacLaren McCann and DDB Toronto. Kyra graduated from the University of Toronto with a degree in Visual Studies. Kyra loves snowboarding and tennis and taking pictures of graffiti.

Yves Lepage Creative Director Magnet Forensics

Senior brand and design professional with unmatched commitment and integrity, offering more than 15 years of successful brand management, creative design and art direction experience. Highly successful in designing and implementing corporate brand strategies, holding all materials to exceptional design standards, as well as establishing strong relationships with stakeholders.

Nick Dingle Senior Product Designer D2L

Nick Dingle has been an interaction designer for over 13 years. He's designed lots of things, from community sites for kids to apps to let you watch movies and TV on your phone. He now works as a Senior Product Designer at D2L.
